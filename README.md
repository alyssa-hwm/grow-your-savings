# 🌱 Savings Calculator

A gentle, beginner‑friendly savings calculator designed to make money feel less overwhelming and more hopeful. This tool helps you estimate how your savings can grow over time through consistent contributions and compound interest (without shame, jargon, or pressure.)

## ✨ Features
- Input your current savings, monthly contributions, and timeline  
- See your projected future balance  
- Breakdown of total contributions vs. interest earned  
- Soft, calming UI designed for readability and emotional ease  
- Mobile‑friendly and lightweight (HTML, CSS, JS only)

## 🌼 Why I Built This
Money tools often feel cold, complicated, or intimidating especially for young creatives or anyone starting from zero. This calculator is part of my “Cozy Finance Playground,” a collection of simple tools that make personal finance feel warm, accessible, and human.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **HTML** for structure  
- **CSS** for the soft, pastel UI  
- **JavaScript** for calculations and animations  
- No frameworks, no backend, no tracking

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Enter how much you already have saved  
2. Add your monthly contribution  
3. Choose your timeline  
4. See your projected savings and interest growth  
5. Adjust inputs to explore different scenarios

## 📦 Running Locally
Download the files and open `index.html` in your browser.  
No setup required.
